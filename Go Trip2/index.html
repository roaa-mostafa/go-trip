The Pyramids

the Pyramids of Giza consist of three main pyramids: the Great Pyramid of Khufu (also known as Cheops), the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure. Each pyramid was constructed as a burial monument for a pharaoh of the Old Kingdom.

Dahab

Dahab's pristine beaches, framed by majestic mountains, boast crystal-clear turquoise waters that lure snorkelers and divers from around the world. The underwater world of Dahab is a treasure trove of vibrant coral reefs teeming with a kaleidoscope of marine life.

Siwa oasis

Siwa Oasis is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, characterized by lush palm groves, shimmering salt lakes, and towering sand dunes. Surrounded by endless stretches of golden desert, the oasis is an oasis in the truest sense.
